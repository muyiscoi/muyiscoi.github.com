I just took and passed the Red Hat Certified System Administrator (RHCSA) exam with a perfect score! (300/300). I was a bit worried as I had not written any lab based certification exam before. I was doubly worried after taking the exam as I just about ran out of time and could not adequately test everything. It all worked out in the end.

With this exam, I'm kicking off the Summer of certifications!. I haven't decided on which one to do next, or that there will even be a next one, so it might turn out to be the "summer of a certification" in the long run. I'm eager to do more though, so we'll just have to see how it goes.

In the mean time, I'm super excited about my new certification and happy to brag about it for a little while longer before looking for a new challenge to tackle.
