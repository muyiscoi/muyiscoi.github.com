The latest version of my favourite Linux distribution,Ubuntu 12.10 aka Quantal Quetzal ,was released last thursday. Despite my Internet connection limitations at present, I have been able to download a copy of the OS.
I'm eager to test out a lot of the new features I have read so much about and also experience the new additions that have made this release so controversial first hand. I would likely post my thoughts on the OS in due time but in the meanwhile, if you're curious about the new version, an excellent review can be found over at The Register.
