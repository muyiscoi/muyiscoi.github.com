Since switching over to an Ubuntu only install on my laptop a few months ago, I've been wondering how I would side load apps on my playbook, cos the method I used required a java app running in windows. Since I didn't really have any app to side load at the time though, I didn't bother looking for a solution. Today, I finally had an app to side load and went about searching for an alternative method of side loading apps for playbook.
I came across "Playbook App Manager". Its an extension for Chrome or Firefox and it works wherever either browser works. I've tested it and it works really well.
